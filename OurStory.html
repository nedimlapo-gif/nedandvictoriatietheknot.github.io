First Date

Ned and Victoria met at Amundsen High School. Victoria taught English and Ned taught Computer Science. A mutual work-friend suggested that the two would, if they ever met, they'd hit it off. While neither of them knew that they'd be married four and a half years later, there was an instant "spark."

Victoria and Ned share a love for basketball. One of their very first dates was to a Chicago Bulls game.

New York Trip #1

A couple of months into their new found relationship, the two played hooky from work to go to New York for an extended weekend.

One could argue that it's not the best idea to go on a weekend-long trip with your new significant other, but Victoria and Ned have said from the get-go that they felt they've been together for a lifetime, even just three months in.

Travel Fever

Another interest that Victoria and Ned share is their love for traveling. The two have visited Puerto Vallarta, New York, Boston, Paris, Amsterdam, and Nashville - to name a few.

Their next big trip is the honeymoon!

New York Trip #2

Victoria and Ned's love came full circle when they returned to the place it all started: New York.

Ned proposed to Victoria under the Manhattan Bridge in February of 2025.

Started our Family

This summer Victoria and Ned took their first step in starting a family by adopting their first dog, Koda.

The two dedicated this past summer to raising their fur baby.
